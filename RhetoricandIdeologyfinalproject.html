Extremism:

        Has an In-group and an out-group

        Action and usually hate - is focused on the out-group, the in-group is a banded and safe group. Loyalty to the in-group is all important.

Types of extremist groups: Racial/Ethnic, Religious, Nationalist, Anti-Government, Anarchist, Classist, Single-Issue Movements, gender/sexual orientation, etc.
